# Question Ideas for the Q&A Feature
## Daily Questions

### For Reflection & Insights

- **Pattern Recognition:** "What patterns did you notice in how I spent my time today?"
- **Energy Audit:** "What activities energized me today versus what drained me?"
- **Value Alignment:** "Based on today's choices, what seemed most important to me?"
- **Hidden Connections:** "What connections do you see between different parts of my day?"
- **Intention Gap:** "What did I say I wanted to do today but never accomplished?"

### For "Aha Moments"

- **Contradiction Spotting:** "Where did my stated intentions and actions not align today?"
- **Insight Mining:** "What insight did I share today that deserves more exploration?"
- **Overthinking Alert:** "Am I overcomplicating any of today's challenges?"
- **Recurring Problems:** "What issue came up multiple times today without resolution?"
- **Gratitude Prompts:** "What positive moment happened today that I didn't fully appreciate?"

### For Quick Memory Recall

- **Commitment Tracker:** "What were the three most important commitments I made today?"
- **Conversation Recap:** "Summarize the key points from my conversation with [person] today."
- **Loose Threads:** "What ideas did I mention today but didn't follow through on?"
- **Resource Collector:** "What resources or recommendations were shared with me today?"
- **Follow-up Reminder:** "Who do I need to follow up with tomorrow and why?"

### For Fun & Positive Reinforcement

- **Joy Tracker:** "What made me laugh or smile today?"
- **Victory Log:** "What small win should I be celebrating from today?"
- **Delight Detector:** "What unexpected pleasant surprise happened today?"
- **Creativity Spotlight:** "What was my most creative moment or idea today?"
- **Gratitude Focus:** "Who had a positive impact on my day that I might want to thank?"

### For Future Planning

- **Micro-Adjustment:** "What small change could make tomorrow better than today?"
- **Curiosity Trail:** "What emerging interest from today deserves more attention?"
- **Momentum Builder:** "What did I start today that I should continue tomorrow?"
- **Time Reclamation:** "What distracted me today that I should minimize tomorrow?"
- **Opportunity Alert:** "What unique opportunity emerged today that I shouldn't miss?"

- **Idea Inventory:** "What ideas or tasks did I mention but never followed up on?"

## Monthly Questions

### For Reflection & Insights

- **Monthly Patterns:** "What patterns emerged in how I allocated my time this month?"
- **Energy Landscape:** "Which activities consistently energized me this month versus drained me?"
- **Value Prioritization:** "Based on where I invested my time this month, what values am I actually prioritizing?"
- **Thematic Connections:** "What themes or connections across different areas of my life became evident this month?"
- **Aspiration Gap:** "Which monthly goals or aspirations did I consistently deprioritize in practice?"

### For "Aha Moments"

- **Behavioral Contradictions:** "What contradictions between my stated priorities and actual behaviors became apparent this month?"
- **Insight Evolution:** "Which insights or ideas evolved most significantly throughout this month?"
- **Solution Obstacles:** "Where might I be creating unnecessary complexity in solving recurring problems?"
- **Progress Blockers:** "What issues came up repeatedly this month without meaningful progress?"
- **Unacknowledged Wins:** "What significant positive developments occurred that I haven't fully recognized?"

### For Quick Memory Recall

- **Key Decision Inventory:** "What were the most consequential decisions I made this month?"
- **Relationship Development:** "How did my key relationships evolve or change this month?"
- **Project Status Summary:** "What's the current status of each major project I worked on this month?"
- **Learning Consolidation:** "What were the most valuable lessons or skills I acquired this month?"
- **Commitment Audit:** "Which commitments did I fulfill, and which did I postpone or abandon?"

### For Fun & Positive Reinforcement

- **Joy Patterns:** "What consistently brought me joy or satisfaction this month?"
- **Achievement Portfolio:** "What accomplishments this month am I most proud of?"
- **Delight Directory:** "What unexpected pleasures or discoveries would I like to experience again?"
- **Creative Output Review:** "What were my most creative or innovative contributions this month?"
- **Gratitude Map:** "Which people had the most positive impact on my month?"

### For Future Planning

- **Monthly Adjustments:** "What three adjustments would make next month more aligned with my priorities?"
- **Habit Evaluation:** "Which habits served me well this month and which should I reconsider?"
- **Focus Refinement:** "Based on this month's experiences, where should I concentrate more energy next month?"
- **Boundary Setting:** "What activities or commitments should I say 'no' to next month?"
- **Opportunity Development:** "Which emerging opportunity from this month has the most potential?"

## Quarterly Questions

### For Reflection & Insights

- **Quarterly Direction:** "What trajectory am I on based on the past three months of activities and choices?"
- **Energy Investment Analysis:** "Where am I investing my energy that delivers the highest return on wellbeing?"
- **Value Evolution:** "How have my priorities and values shifted over the past quarter?"
- **Life Integration:** "How well are the different domains of my life (work, relationships, health, etc.) supporting each other?"
- **Goal Progress Reality Check:** "Which quarterly goals have I authentically progressed on versus merely maintained the illusion of progress?"

### For "Aha Moments"

- **Narrative Contradictions:** "What story am I telling myself that doesn't match the reality of my choices this quarter?"
- **Insight Integration:** "Which insights have I successfully translated into changed behaviors or decisions?"
- **Systematic Obstacles:** "What systems or processes in my life are creating unnecessary friction?"
- **Progress Catalysts:** "What enabled my biggest breakthroughs or progress this quarter?"
- **Growth Blindspots:** "What growth or progress have I made that I'm not giving myself credit for?"

### For Quick Memory Recall

- **Key Milestone Summary:** "What significant milestones or events shaped this quarter?"
- **Relationship Evolution:** "How have my key relationships transformed this quarter?"
- **Project Trajectory:** "Which projects gained momentum and which stalled this quarter?"
- **Knowledge Acquisition:** "What new expertise or skills have I developed this quarter?"
- **Network Expansion:** "Which new connections or relationships formed this quarter might be valuable long-term?"

### For Fun & Positive Reinforcement

- **Joy Analysis:** "What activities consistently brought me the most authentic joy this quarter?"
- **Achievement Review:** "Which achievements this quarter represent meaningful progress toward my larger goals?"
- **Quality Experience Inventory:** "What experiences this quarter would I like to have more of?"
- **Creative Contribution Assessment:** "What did I create or contribute this quarter that I'm most proud of?"
- **Impact Recognition:** "Whose lives did I positively impact this quarter, and how?"

### For Future Planning

- **Quarterly Direction Adjustment:** "What course corrections would align my daily activities better with my larger goals?"
- **Capability Development:** "What capabilities or skills should I prioritize developing next quarter?"
- **Strategic Focus:** "Which 2-3 areas would deliver the highest return on my time and energy next quarter?"
- **Elimination Strategy:** "What should I stop doing entirely next quarter to create space for what matters most?"
- **Opportunity Evaluation:** "Which opportunity presenting itself now has the potential to be most transformative?"

## Yearly Questions

### For Reflection & Insights

- **Annual Arc Review:** "What story does the past year tell about my priorities, growth, and challenges?"
- **Energy ROI Analysis:** "Where did I invest my energy this year that delivered the greatest returns?"
- **Core Value Evaluation:** "How have my fundamental values been expressed or neglected throughout this year?"
- **Life Harmony Assessment:** "How balanced were the different domains of my life this year?"
- **Goal Achievement Reality Check:** "Which annual goals genuinely mattered to me versus those I pursued out of obligation?"

### For "Aha Moments"

- **Life Narrative Examination:** "What story about myself or my capabilities has this year confirmed or disproven?"
- **Transformative Insights:** "What realizations this year fundamentally changed my perspective?"
- **Systemic Limitations:** "What recurring obstacles suggest a need for systematic rather than incremental change?"
- **Breakthrough Analysis:** "What conditions or factors enabled my most significant breakthroughs?"
- **Hidden Progress Recognition:** "What progress have I made this year that hasn't been fully acknowledged?"

### For Quick Memory Recall

- **Defining Moment Inventory:** "What 5-7 moments or decisions most shaped this year?"
- **Relationship Transformation Map:** "How has my relationship landscape evolved this year?"
- **Major Accomplishment Summary:** "What were my most significant accomplishments across all domains of life?"
- **Knowledge Expansion Review:** "How has my understanding of myself and the world expanded this year?"
- **Network Evolution:** "How has my community and network of relationships evolved this year?"

### For Fun & Positive Reinforcement

- **Joy Archeology:** "What consistently brought me deep satisfaction and joy throughout the year?"
- **Legacy Contributions:** "What did I create, build, or contribute this year that I'm most proud of?"
- **Quality Experience Catalog:** "What experiences this year would I want to incorporate more permanently into my life?"
- **Creative Expression Review:** "How did I express my creativity most authentically this year?"
- **Impact Assessment:** "Where and how did I have the most meaningful positive impact this year?"

### For Future Planning

- **Annual Direction Setting:** "What 2-3 themes should define my focus for the coming year?"
- **Capability Roadmap:** "What skills or capabilities would most expand my options and effectiveness next year?"
- **Transformative Focus:** "Which area of my life, if transformed, would most positively affect everything else?"
- **Strategic Elimination:** "What should I courageously let go of to create space for what truly matters next year?"
- **Opportunity Horizon:** "What seedling opportunities from this year have the potential to become forests in the next?"
